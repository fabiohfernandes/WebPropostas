## Decisions Log

### 2025-01-06 - Client Portal Real-World Use Cases
**Context:** Need to demonstrate platform versatility across different industries beyond construction
**Decision:** Added 7 comprehensive use case examples:
1. Home Construction (R$ 500K, 18 months, 12 vendors)
2. Vehicle Body Shop Repair (R$ 18.5K, 45 days, insurance claims)
3. Children's Education & Activities (R$ 24K/year, multiple children)
4. Wedding Planning (R$ 80K, 15 vendors, 150 guests)
5. Home Renovation (R$ 120K, 4 months)
6. Corporate Event Planning (R$ 150K, 500 attendees)
7. Medical Treatment & Healthcare (R$ 32K, 24 months, orthodontics)
**Impact:** Platform now positioned for diverse markets (families, couples, individuals, businesses)
**Status:** Implemented in PRD

### 2025-01-06 - Client Portal & Project Management Hub
**Context:** Need to transform platform from simple proposal tool into complete project management ecosystem
**Decision:** Added Phase 44-45 (8 weeks) - Client Portal with:
- Proposal management dashboard
- Project folders for multi-vendor coordination
- Media library with progress tracking
- Financial management and NFe tax documentation
- Contract repository and obligations tracker
- Complete project memory and audit trail
- Multi-user collaboration with role-based permissions
**Impact:**
- Extends platform value from 6 months to 18-24 months per client
- Targets families managing R$ 100K-1M projects
- Justifies R$ 247/month Professional tier pricing
- Creates competitive moat (no competitor offers this)
**Status:** Implemented in PRD, Phase 44-45 planned

### 2025-01-06 - Advanced Reporting & Campaign Management
**Context:** Need better analytics and client retention features
**Decision:** Added three major feature sets:
1. **Phase 40-41:** Advanced Reporting (client, sector, product reports with export)
2. **Phase 42:** Automated Follow-Up System (tiered reminders via email/WhatsApp)
3. **Phase 43:** Distribution Lists & Campaign Management (segmentation, WhatsApp Business API)
**Impact:**
- Improved client retention through automated engagement
- Revenue analytics for agencies
- Marketing automation for service providers
**Status:** Implemented in PRD, Phases 40-43 planned
