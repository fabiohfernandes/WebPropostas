## SUMMARY

**Total Unique Bullet Types Identified: ~30-35 distinct designs**

**Categories:**
1. Circle-based (with rings, badges, connectors)
2. Chevron/Arrow shapes (various orientations)
3. Card-based (rectangles, rounded, banners)
4. Timeline/Process flow (connecting elements)
5. Target/Bullseye designs
6. Speech bubbles and pins
7. Progress bars and sliders
8. Vertical columns and towers
9. 3D effects (shadows, layers, depth)
10. Donut charts (color schema showcase)

**Common Elements:**
- Icons (from BULLET_ICONS library)
- Numbers (steps, percentages, sequences)
- Text (titles, paragraphs, labels)
- Colors (customizable, schema-based)
- Connectors (lines, arrows, flows)
- 3D effects (shadows, layers)
- Geometric shapes (circles, squares, diamonds, chevrons)

**Implementation Priority:**
Start with simpler designs (circles, squares, basic chevrons) before tackling complex multi-part bullets or 3D effects.
