## 6. Pricing Strategy Tactics

### 6.1 Launch Promotion (First 100 Subscribers)

**"Founding Member" Lifetime Discount:**
- Standard: R$ 77/month (forever) - 20% off
- Professional: R$ 197/month (forever) - 20% off

**Benefits:**
- Creates urgency and FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out)
- Builds loyal early adopter community
- Generates positive word-of-mouth
- Accelerates break-even timeline

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Cap at 100 subscribers maximum
- Limited to first 60 days post-launch
- Grandfather clause for existing Phase 1-19 users (12 months Professional free)

---

### 6.2 Conversion Funnels

**Freemium ‚Üí Standard:**
- **Trigger 1:** After 2nd proposal created, show "Upgrade for AI editing"
- **Trigger 2:** When trying to edit created proposal, show "Upgrade to edit"
- **Trigger 3:** At 10 PDF downloads (out of 10), show "Upgrade for hosted proposals"
- **Trigger 4:** 30-day email nurture campaign highlighting Standard benefits

**Standard ‚Üí Professional:**
- **Trigger 1:** When creating 3rd custom template, show "Upgrade to save templates"
- **Trigger 2:** At 80 proposals (out of 100/month), show "Upgrade for unlimited"
- **Trigger 3:** When adding 9th client (out of 10), show "Upgrade for unlimited clients"
- **Trigger 4:** Monthly email: "Top performing agencies use Professional tier"

---

### 6.3 Churn Prevention

**Early Warning Signals:**
- No login for 14 days ‚Üí Re-engagement email
- No proposals created in 30 days ‚Üí Usage tips email
- Downgrade intent detected ‚Üí Retention offer (1-month discount)

**Win-Back Campaigns:**
- 50% off for 3 months to re-activate cancelled subscribers
- Personal outreach for Professional churned customers
- Exit surveys to understand cancellation reasons

## 9. Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Pricing Risks

**Risk 1: Price Too Low (Revenue Risk)**
- **Indicator:** LTV:CAC < 2:1, profitability below 50%
- **Mitigation:** Annual price increase of 10-15% (grandfathered subscribers exempt)
- **Contingency:** Introduce add-ons (extra AI tokens, premium templates)

**Risk 2: Price Too High (Adoption Risk)**
- **Indicator:** Conversion rate <3%, high bounce on pricing page
- **Mitigation:** A/B test pricing, offer longer trials (30 ‚Üí 60 days)
- **Contingency:** Reduce Standard to R$ 77/month for 6 months

**Risk 3: Freemium Cannibalization**
- **Indicator:** >95% users stay on free tier, <3% conversion
- **Mitigation:** Reduce free tier to 1 proposal lifetime (vs 3)
- **Contingency:** Time-limited free tier (14-day trial vs permanent freemium)

**Risk 4: Payment Processing Issues**
- **Indicator:** >10% failed payment rate
- **Mitigation:** Multiple payment gateways (Stripe + Mercado Pago)
- **Contingency:** Manual payment processing, bank transfer option
